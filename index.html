Shiny Pokémon


Unsurprisingly, there is a much larger portion of non-shiny Pokémon than shiny Pokémon. Not all shiny Pokémon have been released yet, but even the ones that have been are hard to come by. The chance of encountering a shiny Pokémon (excluding during Community Days) is thought to be 1/300 or more. This has fuelled traiding between players as they try to gather as many of the elusive characters as possible.

Egg distances


It is very clear here, that not many Pokémon are available through eggs. Surprisgly, the most amount of Pokémon you can hatch are from 5km eggs - it would have been my thought that there would be more 2km egg Pokémon due to the fact that the most common Pokémon hatch from these eggs.

Pokémon regions

There are 7 regions where Pokémon can originate from, which coincide with the game generations. In the game Pokémon Go, generations 1-3 (Kanto, Johto and Hoenn respectively) have been fully released, with a slow drip release of Gen 4 (Sinnoh) currently taking place.
